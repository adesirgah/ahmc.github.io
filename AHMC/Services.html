Medical Consultation

We offer focused medical consultation service to help patients maintain their health and well-being. By working closely with healthcare providers, patients can achieve better outcomes and enjoy a higher quality of life.

Laboratory Services

A medical laboratory is an integral part of any medical clinic, providing essential diagnostic services that help doctors and other healthcare professionals accurately diagnose and treat a wide range of medical conditions.Our labs are staffed by trained medical professionals who use the latest technologies and techniques to provide accurate and timely results.

General Treatment
  • We seek to create a reliable supply of high quality, low cost, essential drugs and to make them available to the people who need them whenever needed.
  • We seek to treat childhood infectious diseases in the communities; reduce under 5 mortality rates; improve community health thru educational and prevention activities.

Child Welfare Clinic
  • These clinics provide a range of preventive and curative health services, including: growth monitoring, nutritional counseling & treatment of common childhood illnesses.
  • Access to CWC is often subsidized or free, to ensure that all children, regardless of their family's income or insurance status, can receive the care they need.

Antenatal & Postnatal Care
  • It is important to receive antenatal care to ensure a healthy pregnancy and to identify and manage any potential risks or complications that may arise.
  • Postnatal care is important to ensure a healthy recovery for the mother and to monitor the health and development of the newborn.

Minor Surgical Procedures

Minor surgical procedures are common in medical clinics and are often performed to diagnose or treat a variety of conditions. These procedures are usually simple, relatively quick, and can be performed under local anesthesia. They may include:Removal of skin lesions, Incision and drainage, Suturing, Circumcision etc.
